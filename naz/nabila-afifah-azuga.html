About Me

I am a Lecturer in Marine Sciences at the University of Riau, specializing in physical oceanography. My work focuses on air-sea interactions, sea level variability, and climate-driven ocean changes. I am passionate about linking oceanographic research to practical applications in fisheries productivity and coastal resilience. My commitment is to foster transparent ocean monitoring and sustainable marine resource management, contributing to effective ocean-climate mitigation strategies.
